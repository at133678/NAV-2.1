News

West High School student Alexa Christiansen, part of West’s Lemelson-MIT InvenTeam, wrote a grant that awarded West with $10,000 for the 2nd time in three years. Students are working to create a water filtration system for Muamba, Kenya, which will potentially save millions of lives. West is one out of fifteen schools nationwide to receive this grant. They are excited to make a difference by providing a sustainable and inexpensive filtration system out of materials that would be available to villagers in Muamba.
